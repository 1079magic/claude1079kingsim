Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

KingSim ("this tool", "we") is a free, browser-based, client-side strategy helper for KingShot players. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected (spoiler: almost none) and how it is handled.

2. No Personal Data Collected

KingSim does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. There are no user accounts, no login screens, and no registration.

3. Image Processing — Fully On-Device

When you import a screenshot for OCR (text recognition), all image processing happens entirely in your browser using Tesseract.js. Images are never uploaded to any server. They are processed locally in your device's memory and discarded when you close the tab or navigate away.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party analytics service. We do not set tracking cookies. We do not build user profiles.

5. Cookies

KingSim does not set any cookies for tracking or identification purposes. Your browser may set technical cookies required for basic site function (such as Netlify infrastructure cookies), but these are not read or used by KingSim itself.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Netlify — hosts this website. Netlify may log basic server access logs (IP address, page visited, timestamp) for infrastructure and security purposes. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Tesseract.js CDN (jsDelivr) — the OCR library is loaded from a CDN. jsDelivr may log the request. No image data is sent to jsDelivr.
  • Patreon — if you click the Patreon support link, you will be taken to Patreon's website, which has its own privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

KingSim is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. GDPR (EU Users)

Because we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete under GDPR. If you have questions, contact us via the KingShot Kingdom #1079 community channels.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the tool constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

10. Contact

This tool is maintained by the KingShot Kingdom #1079 community. For questions or concerns, reach out via in-game messaging or community channels.
